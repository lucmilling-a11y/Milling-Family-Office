Our Heritage

A family legacy of building and operating businesses in the Ottawa–Gatineau region.

The Milling family brings more than two decades of experience as business founders and operators. Our approach is grounded in the discipline of long-term stewardship, not short-term financial engineering.

Experience Includes
Wakefield Mill Hotel & Spa
Wakefield, Quebec — Co-Founded & Operated by the Milling Family, 2000–2024

For more than two decades, the Milling family was involved in the development and operation of the Wakefield Mill Hotel & Spa in Wakefield, Quebec—transforming a historic 19th-century stone mill into one of the region's most recognized boutique hospitality destinations. The property expanded to include guest accommodations, restaurants, event spaces, and a full service spa.

The business was successfully transitioned to new ownership in 2024. This experience reflects our family's commitment to building businesses with long-term vision, operational discipline, and strong ties to the local community.

We Are Actively Seeking to Acquire a Business

The Milling Family Office is actively seeking to acquire a profitable business in the Ottawa–Gatineau region from an owner considering retirement, succession, or transition.

We are particularly interested in businesses with strong customer relationships, stable operations, and a reputation for quality within their industry.

Owners interested in exploring a potential transition are welcome to contact us confidentially.

Our Approach

How we invest, operate, and build.

Long-Term Ownership

We acquire businesses with the intention of owning them for decades, not years. Our objective is to build upon the foundation established by the current owner while supporting continued growth and stability.

Operator Mindset

Our family has firsthand experience building and operating businesses. This perspective informs how we evaluate opportunities and how we support the companies we acquire.

Local Commitment

We focus on businesses located in the Ottawa–Gatineau region and surrounding communities. Maintaining strong local relationships with employees, customers, and suppliers is central to our approach.

Investment Focus

We focus on established lower middle market businesses that meet the following criteria.

Location

Ottawa, Gatineau, and surrounding regions in Eastern Ontario and Western Quebec.

Business Characteristics

  • Profitable and established operations
  • Strong reputation and loyal customer base
  • 5–40 employees
  • $1M–$10M in annual revenue

Industries of Interest

  • Industrial services
  • Construction and specialty contracting
  • Environmental and technical services
  • Light manufacturing
  • Business-to-business services

For Business Owners

Selling a business is a significant personal and financial decision.

The Milling Family Office offers business owners a transition option focused on continuity, stability, and respect for the legacy they have built.

Unlike traditional private equity firms, we are not driven by a fixed investment timeline. Our goal is to acquire and operate a small number of businesses for the long term.

Our Team

A family of operators, builders, and investors.

RM
Robert Milling
Founder & Operating Partner

Robert is an entrepreneur and hospitality operator best known as the co-founder of the Wakefield Mill Hotel & Spa. In 2000, he partnered to acquire and restore a historic 19th-century stone mill in Wakefield, Quebec, transforming it into a destination inn and spa. Over two decades, Robert expanded the property to include additional accommodations, restaurants, event spaces, and a full service spa. The business was successfully transitioned to new ownership in 2024. Robert continues to advise entrepreneurs and remains actively engaged in the regional business community.

LM
